A Loving Act

NON-PROFIT UI CASE STUDY

Produced a RWD UI solution for a non-profit organization, A Loving Act. Although the website had a simple design, it lacked creativity and credibility; therefore, it was far less inviting aesthetically.

Team: Gisell Giraldo, Amy Brecker, Frederick Smith, Jamesa Hogges, Sheniah Ferguson

Research

We received some awesome feedback from surveys that allowed us critical insight on the target audience, and justified the importance of a brand.
  • People are more willing to donate to charities that resonate with them
  • A strong online presence is a must
  • A cohesive brand can help you stand out from competition and entice consumers to purchase merch
  • Social Media is the main driving force of information

A Loving Act is a nonprofit organization that supports and enhances local communities in the south through three initiatives: homeless outreach, foster kids outreach and prison outreach. While their mission is admirable, we observed that A Loving Act’s purpose is not clear when users arrive at their homepage, which lends to lack of engagement from potential donors and volunteers.

How might we tell A Loving Act’s story in an engaging way through UI design to engage the user and compel him/her to action?
